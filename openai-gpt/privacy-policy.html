Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gatekeeper AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our procurement intelligence platform, including our ChatGPT integration and API services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: API requests, tool invocations, and response metadata for performance monitoring and service improvement.
  • Account information: Name, email address, and organisation name provided during registration or subscription.
  • Document content: Vendor proposals, RFP documents, and bid data submitted for analysis. This content is processed transiently and is not stored beyond the duration of the analysis session unless you explicitly save a report.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our procurement analysis services.
  • To authenticate users and manage subscription access.
  • To monitor API usage and enforce fair-use policies.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate service updates.

We do not use submitted document content to train AI models.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Infrastructure providers (e.g. Railway, Supabase) who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • OpenAI: When you interact with Gatekeeper AI via the ChatGPT platform, your queries are processed under OpenAI's terms and privacy policy.
  • Legal obligations: Where required by applicable law or regulatory authority.

4. Data Retention

Document content submitted for analysis is not retained beyond your active session unless you explicitly request report storage. Account information is retained for the duration of your subscription and for up to 12 months after account closure, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), API key authentication, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Cookies

Our website may use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
